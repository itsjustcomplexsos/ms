More of Who I Am


While most students in school ranted about their endless hatred towards the subject that is Maths, I weirdly enjoyed it. Not the process of doing it - but those 10 seconds when the idea strikes, and followed by them, those adrenalin-filled moments of solving the problem as quickly as possible. I am truly mesmerised by this process. This is the sole reason why I enjoy Mathematics so much.
